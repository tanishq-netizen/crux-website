The Vault

Digital Lookbooks

Product

A private, paywalled archive — lookbooks, guides, breakdowns, templates, whatever format matches your niche — built and kept current under your name, for the audience that already asks how you do it.

What's Inside
  • Content sourcing, writing, and design for the archive itself
  • Paywall, delivery, and access setup on a platform that fits you
  • Ongoing seasonal or rolling updates, so it never goes stale
Best For

Creators whose audience regularly asks "where do you get that" or "how do you actually put this together" — and would happily pay for the direct answer.

Premium Communities

Premium Paid Communities

Service

A private Discord (or similar) space built for the most invested 1–5% of your audience — the ones already DMing you for closer access. We architect it, moderate it, and run it day to day.

What's Inside
  • Server architecture, channel structure, and member rules
  • Vetted onboarding tied to your existing audience
  • Day-to-day moderation, retention, and engagement programming
Best For

Creators with a vocal, loyal core who want closer access to you — without you personally moderating a server every night.

Audits

Monetization & Digital Audits

Strategy

Before anything gets built, we give you a clear, honest read on what your specific audience would actually pay for — and what order to build it in.

What's Inside
  • Audience and content analysis — where trust and attention already concentrate
  • A scan of comparable offers in your category, and where the gaps are
  • A prioritised build plan, which can feed into a Vault or a Community
Best For

Creators who know they're leaving money on the table, but aren't sure where to start — or want a second opinion on an offer they're already running.
